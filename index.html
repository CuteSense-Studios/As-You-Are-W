Meet the Regulars

The heart of the café isn't just the brew—it's the people who stay for a while.

Silvana

The Noble Anchor

"Acceptable. Bram's brews were never this precise."

A noble with a cool exterior. She carries herself like posture is armor, but beneath the surface lies a quiet loyalty.

Dalvana

The Perceptive Dancer

"She creates warmth in rooms because she remembers when they were cold."

Silvana's wife and a local dancer. Genuinely warm and bubbly, she's often the first to make newcomers feel at home.

Guhana

The Steady Soul

"I am used to the confusion. It is fine... you can just be honest with me."

A quiet observer who says exactly what he means. Guhana has a grounded sense of self that is quietly radical.

Yuna

The Joyful Spark

"Loves fiercely and without condition... she simply accepts you as you are."

Excitable and uncomplicated joy. She asks blunt questions but listens intently, providing a necessary emotional anchor.
