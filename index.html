Meet the Regulars

The heart of the café isn't just the brew—it's the people who stay for a while.

Every customer who walks through the worn wooden door of Syth Café carries a story — some are bruised, some are bright, and all of them are searching for something. As the new barista, you'll learn their names, their fears, and the small joys that make them human.

Silvana

The Noble Anchor

"Acceptable. Bram's brews were never this precise. I expected more precision."

A noble with a cool exterior and a complicated interior. She carries herself like posture is armor — but beneath that polished surface lies a quiet loyalty.

✦ First impression: Formal, assessing, slightly intimidating. But watch closely — her rare smiles are worth more than gold. She comes to the café not for the tea, but for the silence it offers.

Dalvana

The Perceptive Dancer

"She creates warmth in rooms because she remembers when they were cold to her."

Silvana's wife and a local dancer. Genuinely warm and bubbly, she is often the first person to make newcomers feel truly welcome.

✦ First impression: Bubbly, bright, immediately at ease. But her cheer isn't naivety — it's a choice. She sees people clearly and chooses to love them anyway.

Guhana

The Steady Soul

"I am used to the confusion. It is fine... you can just be honest with me."

A quiet observer who says exactly what he means. Guhana has a grounded sense of self that is quietly radical.

✦ First impression: Plain, unhurried, slightly hard to read. But his silence isn't empty — it's an invitation. He values truth over performance, and his trust is earned in quiet moments.

Yuna

The Joyful Spark

"Loves fiercely and without condition... she simply accepts you as you are."

Excitable and uncomplicated joy. She asks blunt questions but listens intently, providing a necessary emotional anchor.

✦ First impression: Loud, fast, slightly chaotic, immediately endearing. But beneath the chatter is a bravery that surprises everyone — including herself.

☕ A Place to Belong

In As You Are, you aren't just serving drinks. You're mending a reputation and building a home. Your choices define the very heart of the café.

The Syth Café has seen better days. Its once-warm reputation faded under previous management, but now it's yours to revive. Every cup of masala chai, every mango lassi, every samosa you serve is a chance to write a new chapter. The townsfolk are wary, but a few regulars still wander in — each carrying their own reasons for seeking refuge within these walls. Through quiet conversations and shared silences, you'll discover that belonging isn't something you find... it's something you brew.
